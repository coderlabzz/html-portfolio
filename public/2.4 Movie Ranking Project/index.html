The Best Cyberpunk Movies of All Time

My top 3 movies

1. Blade Runner 2049

This movie is best live action cyberpunk film I have seen to date. It improves on the great visual style of the first Blade Runner movie and has a great story with good plot twists

2. Akira 1988

A great animated film with everything thing you would expect from a good cyberpunk movie. The movie is set in a futuristic Hong Kong and has the gritty high tech low life visual style. It has a good story and lots of good action and it explores many philosophical subjects such as tranhumanism and consciousness

3. The Matrix

This is a great movie that explores many philosophical themes. The story is great and has lots of great action scenes.
